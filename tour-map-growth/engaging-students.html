Seamless testing, powerful insights

Through easy-to-use technology and best-in-class test design, MAP Growth is built to reveal what each student knows—and what they’re ready to learn—when it comes to your standards.

Powered by technology. Driven by authenticity.

Technology-enhanced test questions and well-crafted multiple-choice items allows MAP Growth to authentically assesses your students across multiple depths of complexity.

Developmentally appropriate

With audio support and simple, touch screen-compatible controls, MAP Growth recreates familiar classroom learning tasks for K – 2 students, like using the alphabet to spell a vocabulary word.

Smart solutions to complex problems

Using interactive design, MAP Growth can assess students on abstract concepts difficult to represent with multiple choice. These items also reduce successful guessing, which increases measurement precision.

Go deeper

Pairing adaptive question sets with a single reading passage, MAP Growth mirrors how content is taught in the classroom and assesses students at a deeper level of understanding.

Show what they know

Open response test questions require students to solve problems unaided by multiple choice options, letting students show they understand how to apply key concepts.

Matching the rigor of new standards

Leveraging multi-part questions on key science concepts, MAP Growth can assess for multidimensional understanding required by the Next Generation Science Standards.

A better test experience

With efficient, time-saving set up for educators and simple navigation for students, MAP Growth is a streamlined test experience built to work on any tech set up.

Ready to launch

With MAP Growth, you can launch a new test session for a whole class in just one click. Assign tests or accommodations—and monitor test progress—from one central dashboard.

Simple student set-up

MAP Growth makes test login uncomplicated for students while keeping important security controls—like verifying student identity—easy to manage for proctors.

Ready whenever you are

Use the browser-based platform to administer MAP Growth with confidence, whether remote or onsite and across devices. Access videos, quick-start guides, and best practices collected over nearly a decade of successful remote testing.

Assessments unique as your students

With an adaptive approach, flexible accessibility features, and tools to maximize engagement, every MAP Growth assessment is built specifically for the student taking it.

Meeting students where they are

MAP Growth adapts to each student as they test, homing in on exactly what they know and what they’re ready to learn—regardless of grade level.

The right tools for every student

MAP Growth allows you to assign and track accommodations and accessibility features—like embedded text-to-speech—insuring every student has a test that meets their unique needs.

Engaged students means more reliable results

MAP Growth detects when student start guessing to rush through a test. Live proctor notifications help get students back on track. Student reports let you know if scores are impacted.

Comprehensive standards coverage

With a pool of more than 50,000 high-quality test questions, MAP Growth can assess students on the full breadth—and depth—of your standards in math, reading, English/language arts and science.
